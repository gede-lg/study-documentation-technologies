# Angular 14 → 20 — mudanças importantes p/ desenvolvedores

## v14 (2022-06-02)

- **Typed Reactive Forms**: formulários reativos passam a ser **estritamente tipados** (melhor autocompletar, checagem de tipos, menos `any`). ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v14-is-now-available-391a6db736af?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [Angular](https://angular.dev/guide/forms/typed-forms?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- **Standalone (prévia)**: componentes/diretivas/pipes com `standalone: true`, importando dependências direto no `@Component` (sem NgModule). ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v14-is-now-available-391a6db736af?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- **`inject()`**: função de DI fora do construtor, permitindo helpers/factories funcionais. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v14-is-now-available-391a6db736af?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [DEV Community](https://dev.to/lucaspaganini/whats-new-in-angular-14-91j?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- Extras notáveis: título de página em rotas, melhorias de CLI e CDK. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v14-is-now-available-391a6db736af?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

## v15 (2022-11-16)

- **Standalone estável** em toda a stack (Router/HttpClient/Elements etc.). `provideRouter(...)` e `provideHttpClient(...)` viram o caminho “tree-shakable”. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v15-is-now-available-df7be7f2f4c8))
- **Directive Composition API** (`hostDirectives`): reuso de inputs/outputs/lógica de outras diretivas no host. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v15-is-now-available-df7be7f2f4c8))
- **Guards/Resolvers funcionais** e melhoria em lazy com default export. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v15-is-now-available-df7be7f2f4c8))
- **`NgOptimizedImage`** estável; **Material v15** migra para MDC (ajustes de DOM/CSS). ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v15-is-now-available-df7be7f2f4c8))

## v16 (2023-05-03)

- **Signals (prévia)**: `signal`, `computed`, `effect` + pacote `@angular/core/rxjs-interop` com `toSignal`/`toObservable`. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v16-is-here-4d7a28ec680d))
- **Hydration não-destrutiva (SSR)** em prévia + `provideClientHydration()`. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v16-is-here-4d7a28ec680d))
- **Inputs obrigatórios** (`@Input({ required: true })`). ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v16-is-here-4d7a28ec680d))
- **Router → Inputs**: `withComponentInputBinding()` mapeia dados/params da rota para inputs do componente. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v16-is-here-4d7a28ec680d))
- **Builder esbuild+Vite (prévia)**; **Jest (experimental)**; **config de Zone.js via `provideZoneChangeDetection`**. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v16-is-here-4d7a28ec680d))

## v17 (2023-11-08)

- **Novo fluxo de controle nativo** (blocos `@if`, `@for`, `@switch`) em **prévia**. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/introducing-angular-v17-4d7033312e4b))
- **Deferrable Views** com `@defer` (prévia). ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/introducing-angular-v17-4d7033312e4b))
- **Hydration estável** e pacote **`@angular/ssr`**; prompt/geração SSR/SSG no CLI. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/introducing-angular-v17-4d7033312e4b))
- **Application Builder (Vite+esbuild) por padrão** em apps novos. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/introducing-angular-v17-4d7033312e4b))

## v18 (2024-05-22)

- **Fluxo de controle e `@defer` ficam estáveis**; foco em polimento e **Zoneless (experimental)**. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v18-is-now-available-e79d5ac0affe?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- **Event Replay (prévia)** para SSR/hidratação híbrida. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v18-is-now-available-e79d5ac0affe?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- **Event coalescing habilitado por padrão** em apps novos (Zone.js), reduzindo ciclos de CD — opt-in para projetos existentes. ([Telerik.com](https://www.telerik.com/blogs/angular-18?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

## v19 (2024-11-19)

**Aqui entram as mudanças que você citou** — *inputs/outputs/queries baseados em signals* e mais:

- **Signal Inputs — `input()`**
    
    Forma moderna de declarar inputs: `readonly foo = input<Foo>();`
    
    **Estável em v19**, com **schematic** oficial p/ migrar `@Input()` → `input()`. ([Angular](https://angular.dev/api/router/withComponentInputBinding?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
    
- **Signal Outputs — `output()`**
    
    Substitui `EventEmitter` por uma API signal-first (`readonly saved = output<Item>();` + `saved.emit(value)`). **Estável em v19**. ([justangular.com](https://justangular.com/blog/bind-route-info-to-component-inputs-new-router-feature/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
    
- **Two-way com `model()`**
    
    Nova forma de binding bidirecional orientada a signals: `count = model(0)`. **Estável em v19**. ([Medium](https://medium.com/netanelbasal/binding-router-information-to-routed-component-inputs-in-angular-78ee92f63e64?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
    
- **Queries como signals**
    
    Funções `viewChild()`, `contentChild()`, `viewChildren()` etc. expõem queries como *readonly signals* (sem `@ViewChild` mutável). **Estável em v19**. ([Angular](https://angular.dev/guide/components/queries?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
    
- **SSR/Hidratação**
    
    **Event Replay fica estável** (reproduz eventos do usuário capturados antes da hidratação). ([Angular](https://angular.dev/api/platform-browser/withEventReplay?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
    
    **Incremental/Partial Hydration (prévia)** com `withIncrementalHydration()` e **gatilhos `hydrate`** em blocos `@defer`. ([Ninja Squad](https://blog.ninja-squad.com/2024/11/19/what-is-new-angular-19.0/?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [Angular](https://angular.dev/guide/incremental-hydration?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
    
- **DX**
    
    **HMR**: estilos por padrão em 19.0; **templates por padrão em 19.1**. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/meet-angular-v19-7b29dfd05b84?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [Ninja Squad](https://blog.ninja-squad.com/2025/01/15/what-is-new-angular-19.1/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
    
    **Refactors no Language Service** (converter inputs/outputs para signals direto no editor). ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/mgechev_angular-typescript-javascript-activity-7252681624906080256-Wc4X?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
    

### Antes vs. depois — inputs/outputs/queries em signals

```tsx
// <= v18 (clássico)
@Input()  userId!: string;
@Output() saved = new EventEmitter<User>();
@ViewChild(MatMenuTrigger) trigger!: MatMenuTrigger;

// v19+
readonly userId = input.required<string>();
readonly saved  = output<User>();
readonly trigger = viewChild(MatMenuTrigger);

```

```tsx
// Two-way binding com model()
@Component({ /* ... */ })
export class Counter {
  count = model(0);
  inc() { this.count.update(v => v + 1); }
}
// <app-counter [(count)]="parentCount"></app-counter>

```

## v20 (2025-05-28)

- **Signals — estabilizações importantes**: `effect`, **`linkedSignal`**, `toSignal` e **`toObservable`** viram **estáveis** (ponte RxJS↔signals madura). ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/announcing-angular-v20-b5c9c06cf301?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [Angular Courses](https://www.angular.courses/new-in-angular/20/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- **Zoneless change detection (dev preview)** com `provideZonelessChangeDetection()`; integração com testes e hooks de render mais claros (`afterEveryRender`, `afterNextRender` estáveis). ([Angular](https://angular.dev/api/core/provideZonelessChangeDetection?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [Angular.love](https://angular.love/angular-20-whats-new?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- **Templates mais expressivos**:
    
    — **Template literals** sem tag (``col-${n}`` em bindings)
    
    — **Operador de exponenciação `**`**, **`in`**, suporte a **`void`** em handlers
    
    — **Template HMR por padrão**. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/announcing-angular-v20-b5c9c06cf301?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [Angular](https://angular.dev/guide/templates/expression-syntax?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [LogRocket Blog](https://blog.logrocket.com/angular-v20-update/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
    
- **DevTools/Perf**: **trilha Angular no Chrome DevTools** (integração nativa de profiling) além do Angular DevTools. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/announcing-angular-v20-b5c9c06cf301?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- **Deprecations alinhadas ao novo template**: `NgIf`/`NgFor` etc. marcados como **deprecated em v20** (substituir por `@if`/`@for`); intenção de remoção em v22. ([Angular](https://angular.dev/api/common/NgIf?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

## Impacto prático e recomendações

1. **Adote inputs/outputs/model baseados em signals (v19+)**
    - Mais seguros (readonly), sem race conditions de setters; interop direta com `signal()/computed()/effect()`.
    - Use a **migration** para converter `@Input()` → `input()` e refatore `EventEmitter` → `output()`. ([Angular](https://angular.dev/reference/migrations/signal-inputs?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
2. **Queries como signals**
    - Troque `@ViewChild` mutável por `viewChild()` imutável; melhora a previsibilidade de leitura/reatividade. ([Angular](https://angular.dev/guide/components/queries?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
3. **SSR + Hidratação moderna**
    - Em apps com SSR, ative **Event Replay** (v19) e avalie **Incremental Hydration** (v19+ prévia; v20 com docs e polimento). ([Angular](https://angular.dev/api/platform-browser/withEventReplay?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
4. **Construa no caminho do futuro**
    - Use **@if/@for** (v17→v18 estável) e planeje remover `NgIf/NgFor`. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v18-is-now-available-e79d5ac0affe?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [Angular](https://angular.dev/api/common/NgIf?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
    - Garanta **builder Vite+esbuild** (v17 padrão) e **HMR de templates** ativo (v19.1+/v20). ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/introducing-angular-v17-4d7033312e4b), [Ninja Squad](https://blog.ninja-squad.com/2025/01/15/what-is-new-angular-19.1/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
5. **Zoneless (quando fizer sentido)**
    - Em projetos novos/performance-sensiveis, experimente **Zoneless** (dev preview) — reduz acoplamento com Zone.js e simplifica stacktraces; verifique compatibilidade com libs. ([Angular](https://angular.dev/api/core/provideZonelessChangeDetection?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

## TL;DR — os “grandes blocos” desde a v14

- **Standalone → estável (v15)** e **builder Vite+esbuild (v17)**. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v15-is-now-available-df7be7f2f4c8))
- **Reatividade com Signals**: prévia (v16) → **controle de template/defers estáveis (v18)** → **inputs/outputs/model/queries em signals estáveis (v19)** → **effect/linkedSignal/toSignal/toObservable estáveis (v20)**. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/angular-v16-is-here-4d7a28ec680d), [Angular](https://angular.dev/api/router/withComponentInputBinding?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [justangular.com](https://justangular.com/blog/bind-route-info-to-component-inputs-new-router-feature/?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [Medium](https://medium.com/netanelbasal/binding-router-information-to-routed-component-inputs-in-angular-78ee92f63e64?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- **SSR/Hidratação**: hydration estável (v17), **Event Replay estável (v19)**, **Incremental Hydration (v19 prévia, evoluindo em v20)**. ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/introducing-angular-v17-4d7033312e4b), [Angular](https://angular.dev/api/platform-browser/withEventReplay?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
- **Templates**: `@if/@for` → padrão; **literals/`*`/`in`/`void` e **HMR por padrão** (v20). ([Angular Blog](https://blog.angular.dev/announcing-angular-v20-b5c9c06cf301?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [Angular](https://angular.dev/guide/templates/expression-syntax?utm_source=chatgpt.com), [LogRocket Blog](https://blog.logrocket.com/angular-v20-update/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))
